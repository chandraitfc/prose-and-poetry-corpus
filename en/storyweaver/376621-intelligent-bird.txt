They were discussing about who will be the queen of all the birds in forest. "My question is who are the important and respectable people for a king or queen " said the owl. Some said friends of the king or queen. But Ms. Magpie said that answer should be the people of that country or state. The owl said "Ms. Magpie is saying the correct answer. So, she will be the queen of all birds. This is how Ms. Magpie became the queen of all birds in that forest.
